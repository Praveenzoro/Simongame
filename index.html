HOW TO PLAY

Welcome to the Memory Power Game! Press "Start" to begin.
Level 1: A random color will appear. Remember this color and tap it.
Level 2: After a short delay, another random color will appear. You need to tap both the color from Level 1 and the new color. so for the next level you need to tap colours of level 1 + level 2 + level 3 random color and it goes on...
Continue this process as you progress through the levels. By Level 20, you should be able to recall the entire sequence of colors from each level.
